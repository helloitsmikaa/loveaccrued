3 to 5 business days within the US. You will get a confirmation email when your order ships.

At least 3 feet long depending on the number of items. It arrives rolled up in a premium gift box. Think CVS receipt energy but make it personal.

Yes. Enter their address at checkout and it ships straight to their door. You can also have it sent to yourself first.

The more detail you give us the better the output. If you are not satisfied with your digital receipt, email us and we will regenerate it.
